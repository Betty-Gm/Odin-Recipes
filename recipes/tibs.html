Tibs (Ethiopian Sauteèed Meat)

Tibs is a popular Ethiopian dish made of sautéed meat usually beef or lamb cooked with onions, peppers, and aromatic spices. It's flavorful, quick to prepare, and often served sizzling hot, making it a favorite for both everyday meals and celebrations.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb beef or lamb, cut into small cubes
  • 2 onions, sliced
  • 2 green peppers, sliced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp niter kibbeh or butter
  • 1-2 tsp berbere spice(optional)
  • 1 rosemary sprig (optional, traditional touch)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Heat niter kibbeh or butter in a hot skillet or pan.
  2. Add meat cubes and sear on high heat until browned on all sides.
  3. Add onions and garlic; sauté until onions soften.
  4. Add green peppers and tomatoes; cook for 5-7 minutes.
  5. Stir in berbere spice (if using) and rosemary.
  6. Season with salt and pepper; cook until meat is done to your liking.
  7. Serve hot, traditionally with injera or bread.
