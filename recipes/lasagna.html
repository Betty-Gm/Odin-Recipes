Lasagna

Lasagna is a classic Italian baked pasta dish made of layered sheets of pasta, rich meat sauce, and creamy cheese. It's a comforting, hearty meal loved worldwide for its combination of savory tomato sauce, melted cheese, and soft pasta layers baked to golden perfection.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 24 oz tomato sauce
  • 15 oz ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt, pepper, Italian seasoning to taste

Steps

  1. Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions; drain and set aside.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan, sauté onion and garlic until soft.
  3. Add ground beef, cook until browned. Stir in tomato sauce and seasoning; simmer 15 minutes.
  4. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, egg, and half the parmesan.
  5. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  6. Layer: meat sauce, noodles, ricotta mixture, mozzarella, repeat layers.
  7. Top with remaining mozzarella and parmesan.
  8. Cover with foil, bake 25 minutes; uncover, bake 15 more minutes until golden.
  9. Let rest 10 minutes before slicing.
